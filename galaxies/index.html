OUTPOST 77

Twin Suns Ranch

Lancaster, CA

Outer-galaxy cosplay experience

Saturday June 28th, 2025 at 5pm

The Event

Step into the gritty Galactic Rim and experience the ultimate Galactic adventure with fully costumed cosplayers and a fully-themed environment at Outpost 77.

You are your own character in an outpost in a galaxy far, far away. This means aliens or creatures of all species and sorts: smugglers, pilots, gangsters, bounty hunters, criminals, etc.

Costumes are strongly encouraged, and can be elaborate or incredibly simple. This elevates the experience for you and others attending. Think vests, khaki jackets, ponchos, capes, straps, goggles, etc. A lot of looks you'd see in a Western would work. Wasteland gear works. Earth-toned desert garb is simple, and a little goes a long way... Please avoid items that would look out of place in this setting, such as ball caps, printed text, logos, plaid, etc.
