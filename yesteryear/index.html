YESTERYEAR

Stanislav Keep

Turlock, CA

Fully-immersive 14th to 15th Century Reenactment

April 10-13, 2025

The Event

Yesteryear is a 14-15th century reenactment where participants dress in their favorite time-appropriate costumes. Experience live-action combat, archery that will test your strength and accuracy, and live entertainment.

Longbow

Loose arrows in an archery competition with the help of the California Warbow Society.

The Knights

See live combat with spear, sword, and axe.

Crossbow

Shoot the deadly weapon banned by the Church in 1139.

The Gallery

Experience tournaments up close from our period box seats.

Feast

Experience dining in our Great Hall with our dining pass.

Sweets

Indulge yourself with handcrafted desserts.

Sir Charles

Raise a pint to live music at Sir Charles' Tavern.

The Campfire

Learn to live as your ancestors did over 500 years ago.

Rest and Relax

Camp onsite or rent one of our period rooms.

King and Queen Suite

Live like royalty in our finest room fit for a King.

Lords and Ladies Rooms

Stay in one of our spacious rooms with views of the village

The Wildlife

See the animals in their native environment.

The Village

Go back in time during this fully-immersive weekend.

About Us

Our team of set designers, actors, and amateur archeologists seek to give participants a chance to glimpse into bygone eras. We build fully-immersive experiences where participants can discover a world more richly colorful and handsomely detailed than many might imagine.
